The Lords and Ladies at House DKT tasked us to deliver a digital campaign that is informative, educative and can encourage all sexually active women, married and unmarried to learn about their product (Lydia IUD) and choose it as a preferred pregnancy prevention/family planning option.

what we did

There is little or no understanding of the IUD in Nigeria, thereby causing a low adoption rate especially by the average or rural woman. This insight is what birthed the #BeLydiaSmart campaign. The #BeLydiaSmart is a consumer education and awareness campaign to inform the audience about the Lydia IUD, its use, advantages over other birth control methods, shun myth & misconception and ultimately drive consideration and adoption of the brand as the most preferred contraceptive method in Nigeria.
